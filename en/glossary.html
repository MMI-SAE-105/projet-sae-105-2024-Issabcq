Glossary

Artificial Intelligence (AI) :
Artificial intelligence refers to the ability of a computer system or machine to simulate human cognitive functions, such as learning, reasoning, language comprehension and problem solving. It uses algorithms and data models to process information and make autonomous decisions..
Robotics :
La Robotics is the branch of engineering and science that designs, builds, programs and uses robots. It encompasses the study of automated systems capable of performing tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously, often by imitating human actions or interacting with their environment..
Sentience :
La Sentience refers to a being's ability to perceive, feel and experience emotions or sensations. It involves self-awareness and sensitivity to internal and external stimuli, enabling us to experience pain, pleasure and other emotional states..
Humanoid :
Un A humanoid is a robot or artificial entity that imitates the shape, characteristics and sometimes behavior of human beings. These robots can have an appearance similar to that of humans, with facial features, limbs and posture that evoke human anatomy..
